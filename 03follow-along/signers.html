Who Were the Signers

Who Were the Notable Signers

John Hancock
Representative from Massachusetts; his large signature made his name synonymous with "signature" in American culture.
Thomas Jefferson
Representative from Virginia; the primary author of the Declaration of Independence.
Benjamin Franklin
Representative from Pennsylvania; a prominent statesman, inventor, and diplomat.
John Adams
Representative from Massachusetts; a strong advocate for independence and later the second U.S. president.
Samuel Adams
Representative from Massachusetts; known for his leadership in the American Revolution.
Richard Henry Lee
Representative from Virginia; initially proposed the motion for independence.
Roger Sherman
Representative from Connecticut; a member of the Committee of Five that drafted the Declaration.
